Mahjong - Classic Chinese Tile Matching Game

Challenge your memory and concentration with this traditional tile-matching puzzle game that has captivated players for centuries.

How to Play Mahjong

Mahjong is a tile-matching puzzle game that originated in China during the Qing dynasty. The goal is to match and remove all tiles from the board.

  • Match identical tile pairs to remove them from the board
  • Only "free" tiles can be matched (tiles not blocked by others)
  • Plan your moves carefully to avoid getting stuck
  • Clear all tiles from the board to win the game

Game Features

  • Hint system to help when you're stuck
  • Shuffle option when no moves are available
  • Track your time and number of moves
  • Relaxing background music and sound effects

About Mahjong

Mahjong is one of the most popular traditional games from China, with a history dating back over 150 years. Originally played with physical tiles as a four-player game, the solitaire version has become incredibly popular worldwide as a puzzle game.

The game features tiles with various Chinese characters and symbols, including circles, bamboo sticks, dragons, winds, and flowers. Players must use strategy and careful observation to match pairs of identical tiles that are "free" (not blocked by other tiles).

This digital version of Mahjong solitaire brings the classic game to your browser, allowing you to enjoy this timeless puzzle anywhere, anytime. Challenge yourself to improve your time and develop strategies to clear even the most difficult layouts.
